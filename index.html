Welcome to Pine Top

Nestled among longleaf and shortleaf pines, Pine Top Campground offers a quiet, back-to-nature experience with easy access for both RVs and tents. Expect roomy sites, friendly hosts, and plenty of fresh air.

  • Shaded campsites with privacy
  • Designated fire rings & firewood
  • Pads for small to mid-size RVs
  • Walk-in and drive-up tent sites

Season & Hours

Open: Year-round

Quiet Hours: 10pm–7am

Pet Friendly: Yes (leash required)

Good to Know

  • Cell service is spotty—enjoy the unplugged time.
  • Pack out what you pack in.
  • Fire conditions may change—check signs at arrival.

Amenities

Water Access

Shared potable water spigots near central loop.

Vault Toilets

Clean and serviced regularly.

Firewood

Bundles available for purchase at the host site.

Trash & Recycling

Pack-it-out encouraged; limited receptacles at exit.

Trail Access

Footpaths to overlooks and a small creek.

Dark Skies

Milky Way views on clear nights.

Location

We’re tucked in the pines of Tennessee, within an hour’s drive of small towns, rivers, and scenic overlooks. Detailed directions are sent with your booking.

  • Gravel access road—passable for most vehicles in dry conditions.
  • Nearest fuel & groceries within 20 minutes.
  • Trailheads right from camp.
